1. iPhone X

As far as flagships go, the Apple iPhone X is the best phone in 2018. The phone offers a power packed performance, thanks to the Apple A11 Bionic chip and also has one of the best cameras we have encountered this year.

Specifications : Apple iPhone X
Ram & Storage 3 GB | 64 & 256 GB
Display 5.8 (1125 x 2436)
Processor Hexa
Operating System iOS
Primary Camera 12 MP & 12 MP
Soc Apple A11 Bionic

2. Samsung Galaxy Note 8

The Samsung Galaxy Note 8 brings the perfect combination of premium design and high end features. It’s not faster than the iPhone 8 Plus, but the best smartphone out there is not about speed alone.

Specifications : Samsung Galaxy Note 8
Ram & Storage 6 GB | 64 GB
Display 6.3 (1440 x 2960)
Processor 2.3 GHz,Octa
Operating System Android
Primary Camera 12 MP & 12 MP
Soc Exynos 8895

3. Google Pixel 2 XL

The Google Pixel 2 XL had a rough start, but Google promptly fixed the display issues and hence, the Pixel 2 XL secures its place in this list of top phones of 2018. The device quite simply the best pictures you can click via a smartphone right now.

Specifications : Google Pixel 2 XL
Ram & Storage 4 GB | 64/128 GB
Display 6 (1440 x 2880)
Processor 2.4 GHz,Octa
Operating System Android
Primary Camera 12 MP
Soc Qualcomm Snapdragon 835

4. Samsung Galaxy S8+

For those who like the big screen more, the Samsung Galaxy S8+ brings a 6.2 inch display and QHD resolution. The AMOLED panel looks good and the Exynos 8895 SoC keeps things fast and smooth. With the same camera as the Galaxy S8, this is basically for those looking for an Android smartphone with a big screen.

Specifications : Samsung Galaxy S8+
Ram & Storage 4 GB | 64/128 GB
Display 6.2 (1440 x 2960)
Processor 2.3 GHz,Octa
Operating System Android
Primary Camera 12 MP
Soc Exynos 8895

5. Apple iPhone 8+

The newest version of the iPhone is not a big update over its predecessor, but given that last year’s iPhone has ruled this list so far, the iPhone 8 Plus has to be here. The iPhone 8 Plus has one of the best cameras on the market and it’s the fastest smartphone you can find today.

Specifications : Apple iPhone 8+
Ram & Storage 3 GB | 64 GB & 256 GB
Display 5.5 (1080 x 1920)
Processor Hexa
Operating System iOS
Primary Camera 12 MP & 12MP
Soc Apple A11 Bionic

6. Oneplus 5T

The OnePlus 5T is without a doubt the fastest Android smartphone on the market today. However, it’s not quite the “flagship killer” that the company has been known for. OnePlus’ dual-camera algorithms aren’t quite flagship class, but the phone still has a great camera.

Specifications : Oneplus 5T
Ram & Storage 6 GB | 64 GB
Display 6 (1080 x 2160)
Processor 2.45 GHz,Octa
Operating System Android
Primary Camera 6 + 20 MP
Soc Snapdragon 835
