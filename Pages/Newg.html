This may be Google's next big bet in the hardware industry

Updated: Dec 8, 2017, 05.34PM IST

Google might be planning to try its luck in the console market with subscription-based gaming. The search giant, which is not quite successful in the smartphone, tablet or speaker industry for now, could soon be locking horns with Microsoft and Sony as the duo are major players in the particular field. By extension it may also be going against Apple, Nintendo and Amazon.The firm is trying to make this happen by making the subscription-based gaming service available via its Chromecast supporting devices, says The Information. The firm could also be working on a hardware component as well.



PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds reveal new Miramar map

Updated: Dec 8, 2017, 05.34PM IST

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, or PUBG as it is better known, has unveiled a new exciting addition. At the Game Awards event, PUBG announced that it is adding a new map Miramar - a welcome addition for the gamers. Earlier, it had the Erangel map and the new map promises to hold new secrets for players to discovers. PUBG has been one of the most popular games on Steam in 2017 and it was revealed that Mirmar would be available on PC testing servers from today. The video that was showcased reveals the Miramar is unlike Erangel and is set in a desert. Erangel, on the other hand, was full of lush green fields and forest. What was also revealed is that there will be pickup truck vehicle available for combatants to cross the terrain with relative ease.



Assassin's Creed Origins to be launched in India on October 26

Updated: Dec 8, 2017, 05.34PM IST

ssassin's Creed Origins, one of the most anticipated games of the year, will be launched in India on October 26. e-xpress Interactive, one of the leading distributor for video games, has announced that gamers who want to buy the game or have pre-ordered the game can get their copies in Mumbai. The game will be available at two stores: The Games Shop in Goregaon and Jai Ganesh store in Thane. Assassin's Creed Origins is officially releasing for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on October 27. The Standard Edition is priced at ₹3599 while the Deluxe Edition is priced at ₹3799. Assassin's Creed Origins Gods Edition is priced at 7999. Assassin's Creed Origins is also available for pre-order with leading retailers, both online and offline.



20 years of GTA: The series that reigns supreme

Updated: Oct 8, 2017, 11.11AM IST

Twenty years ago this month Grand Theft Auto or better known as GTA was released for the first time. It wouldn't be an exaggeration to say that action- adventure games have never been the same since then. GTA had the perfect mix of spectacular and simplicity - one of the reasons why the game still remains widely popular. It had all the elements which a gamer looks for into a game: challenging, replayable and, of course, memorable. The latest version of the game - GTA V - has sold more than 80 million copies and remains the number one video game in the UK. It was first released for PC, PlayStation and GameBoy in October 1997. The first version of the game was set across three fictional cities in the US. Such was the impact that many outlets called for an outright ban of the game as it was percieved to be too violent. As a player you had to choose a mission to progress and GTA had everything - from car chase, racing, underworld characters - that makes a game exciting to play.
