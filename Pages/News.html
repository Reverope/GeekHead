Microsoft launches cheaper models of Surface Book 2 and Surface Laptop

Updated: Feb 3, 2018, 03.54PM IST

Microsoft is making its last year's Surface Laptop and the Surface Book 2 slightly more affordable by introducing a cheaper variant for both. The Microsoft Surface laptop, which came out in May last year alongside Windows 10 , had a starting price of $999. But now, the laptop can be purchased starting $799. The new variant sports Intel Core m3 processor along with Intel HD Graphics 615, 4GB RAM and 128GB inbuilt storage. For reference, the $999 model sports Intel Core i5 processor, 4GB/8GB RAM options and 128GB inbuilt storage. The $1,599 variant sports Intel Core i7 processor alongside 8GB/16GB RAM options and 256GB inbuilt storage.



Asus announces new range of laptops at CES 2018

Updated: Feb 3, 2018, 03.54PM IST

Like other tech giants Acer and Dell, Asus has also showcased its latest range of laptops and AIOs at the ongoing tech show CES 2018. The company has unveiled ZenBook 13, ZenBook Flip 14, ROG G703, X507, Vivo AiO V272 and Vivo AiO V222 at the event. Along with this, the company also announced its Windows Mixed reality headset. The Asus ZenBook 13 comes with magnesium-aluminum alloy design and it runs Windows 10 operating system with support for Windows Hello and Cortana digital assistant. The device is powered by 8th Generation Intel Core i7 processor paired with 16GB of RAM and 1TB SSD. Weighing 985 grams the laptop comes with Harman Kardon speakers which promises to offer immersive sound experience.

Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro laptop with Intel 8th-Gen processor launched

Updated: Feb 3, 2018, 03.54PM IST

Xiaomi has launched its Apple MacBook Pro rival at an event in Beijing, China. Dubbed Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro, the laptop comes in three variants based on processor and RAM combination. All three models come with Intel 8th gen 'Kaby Lake-R' processors. The Core i7+16GB RAM version costs RMB 6999, Core i7+8GB RAM costs RMB 6399 and the Core i5+8GB RAM is priced at RMB 5599. The Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro comes with a 15.6-inch narrow display with 72% NTSC colour gamut. The screen comes protected with Corning Gorilla Glass 3 and Harman Infinity custom built speakers supporting Dolby Atmos audio. The Xiaomi Mi Notebook Pro comes with 2-antenna AC Wi-Fi for better connectivity. There's a fingerprint sensor as well that teams up with Windows Hello security feature. It also includes a 3-in-1 SD card SLOT along with USB Type-C port to charge the device and transfer data. Xiaomi says the laptop comes with 60Wh battery that supports quick charge technology as well.



Dell showcases XPS 15 2-in-1, VR-ready Inspiron gaming desktop and Dell Cinema at CES 2018

Updated: Feb 3, 2018, 03.54PM IST

Apart from showcasing the refreshed lineup of its Latitude 5000 and 7000 series, Dell has also announced XPS 15 2-in-1 laptop along with Dell Cinema audio-visual experience and VR-ready Dell Inspiron gaming desktop at CES 2018, in Las Vegas, US. The Dell XPS 15 2-in-1 laptop comes with a 15.6-inch Infinity Edge 4K Ultra HD and a 15.6-inch full HD display models. The device is powered by 8th generation Intel Core processor with Intel HD graphics and Radeon RX Vega M graphics. The convertible flaunts a maglev keyboard with a bezel-less display and runs Windows 10 operating system. The laptop also comes with Xbox app support for DirectX12 and enables the gamers to directly stream their gameplay using Mixer. The company has priced the device at $1,299.99 and it will be available in US starting April this year. Customers can go to the official website of the company in order to purchase the device.



MWC 2017: Porsche Design launches Book One 2-in-1 Windows 10 Pro laptop

Updated: Feb 3, 2018, 03.54PM IST

The Germany-based sports and lifestyle brand Porsche Design showcased its new laptop, called Book One, at the ongoing Mobile World Conference 2017 (MWC 2017) in Barcelona, Spain. The 2-in-1 Windows 10 Pro laptop is priced at $2,495 and can be termed as the company's alternative to Surface Book. The Porsche Design Book One runs Windows 10 operating system and features a 13.3-inch QHD display with 3200x1800 pixel resolution. The laptop flaunts an aluminium body with matte anodized surface finish. It comes with a hinge which enables the tablet unit to rotate full 360 degrees. The laptop is powered by an Intel Core i7 processor paired with 16GB RAM and 512GB SSD storage. It has two USB Type C ports and two USB 3.0 ports. It also features a built-in USB 3.1 Type-C port. The company has also incorporated a digital Stylus which can be magnetically attached to the device.
