1. HP SPECTRE X360 ($1649)

HP Spectre x360 is a stylish and powerful Everyday Laptop and is powered by Intel Core i5 (7th Gen ) processor clocked at a speed of 2.5 GHz.On the memory front, the laptop is equipped with a hard drive of 360 GB and a 8 DDR3 RAM,thereby making it possible to store ample amount of data.

Specifications
Ram & Storage 32GB DDR4, 2400 MHz
Display 1920x1080
Processor Intel Core i5 (7th Gen)
Graphic Processor NVIDIA GeForce MX150 (2GB GDDR5)

2. Asus ROG GX800 ($2270)

3. Acer Nitro 5 ($748)

The Nitro 5 is all about making gaming affordable and even though we have small niggles about the device, it is still a good entry level gaming laptop. With an Intel Core i5-7300HQ processor and GeForce GTX 1050 GPU inside, the Acer Nitro 5 will allow you to game at 1080p at a steady 60fps as long as you keep the graphics settings at medium or on auto.

Specifications
Ram & Storage 8GB DDR4
Display 15.6”, Full HD (1920 x 1080), IPS
Processor Intel Core i5-7300HQ
Graphic Processor NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 (4GB GDDR5)
Operating System Windows 10 Professional
